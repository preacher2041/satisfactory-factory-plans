Extraction & routing

Access to 1 Pure, 2 Normal, and 1 Impure Crude Oil node. Demand (364.5 m³/min) is well under the combined 100%-clock capacity of all four nodes (540 m³/min), so only two extractors run: the Pure node clocked to 105% feeds the Plastic bank exactly (252 m³/min), and one Normal node clocked to 93.75% feeds the Rubber bank exactly (112.5 m³/min). Fluids route through Pipeline Junctions rather than belt splitters, so each bank is a natural manifold — every refinery in a bank pulls an equal share since all machines in that bank share the same clock speed.

Heavy Oil Residue can't be sunk directly, so both banks' HOR byproduct routes to a dedicated Petroleum Coke bank on Floor 2: the Rubber bank's 75/min HOR feeds it as an internal same-floor feed, and the Plastic bank's 84/min HOR arrives via pipeline lift from Floor 1 below.
