Load balancer sub-floor layout

Each LB sub-floor is 8m tall (2 wall stacks) and sits directly below the production floor it feeds. Belt lifts on the exterior wall carry materials up into the machine floor above. This keeps every production floor clean — no splitter trees competing with machines.
