Ore budget

Three normal Mk.2 miners overclocked to 133.3% each supply exactly 240 ore/min total — 15 ore/min more than the smelters consume at 93.75% clock (225/min). The smelters self-regulate: they run at 93.75% because the ingot demand downstream is exactly 225/min. The 15 ore/min excess simply isn't pulled through.

133.3% sits between 100% and 150% so each miner requires exactly 1 power shard. No second shard is needed on any of them.
